Introduction

‚ñº

Lenda Sarieh (IPA:/lend…ô sa:li'e…™/) is a constructed language which is created by Jack Wang, a conlanger since the year 2010. It's an independent conlang from natural languages which absorbs the essence of languages from all over the world.
In the autumn of 2010, Jack Wang started his conlanging. This conlang was named "Lenda Sarieh" in 2012, which means "a language whose learning cost is dirty-cheap".
In 2018, Jack Wang added elements of vocabulary and grammar in Chinese(including its dialects such as Cantonese and Hokkien), English, Japanese, Korean, Vietnamese, Malay, etc. to the original Lenda Sarieh language, boosting its further development.

Settings

‚ñº

Lenda Sarieh is an analytic language whose word order is VOS. With its unique and clear syntatic structure, and multiple words derived from natural languages, the learning cost of this conlang will decrease.
The development of Lenda Sarieh language will last forever. Welcome to support and join the Lenda Sarieh Community, and contribute to Lenda Sarieh language!
