Mung Jah (文字) Satoka

▼

Induree Jot (秋) 2010, Sutatoh Chua Jeh (實業) Lenda Sequrah, aetah Jack Wang. Myung Sam (聲名) "Lenda Sarieh", Lenda Kah, Induree 2012, Cuk Yet (詞義) "Lenda 1, Kuk Ch'ah Verkuh Tei Doh (估價 Verkuh 低多)".
IDR 2018, Satekuh Cuk Mung Teh Doh, Lenda Chung Hwa (中華) [Poh Kong (包容) Lenda Tei (地) 'te Kwang Tok (廣東), Hok T'en (福建) Doh], Yung (英), Nippon (日本), Koryo (高麗), Viet Nam (越南), Melayu ATK [ata Saekan (時間) Kareneh], Tok (度) Lenda Sarieh Ch'ok Tai (初態), Suah Tuk (速度) Seung Ket (生計) Sya (下) Aeto, aetah Jack Wang.
